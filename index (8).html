About Us

Quantech Origin founded by Mr. Ananth P Mallya , in 2007 , is a unique and novel concept which creates an innovative amalgamation of corporate and young employable engineers , and thereby aiding to India’s progress in globalization. QuanTech is adept in training solutions that not just up-skill a student but also widens their knowledge horizon. Our expertise includes strengthening behavioral skills and enriching analytical and logical thinking. Over the years, we have emanated as one of the pre-eminent assessment and training organizations of India.


We are a zealous team of highly qualified professionals in the field of IT sector and recruitments, with proficient experience in training since 2007. At Quantech we are a highly focused team to provide an exemplary education , training and resourcing services with a distinct emphasis on enhancing the significance of ‘flexible resourcing’.




Our Services

Recruitment Service

QuanTech Origin is the pioneer of organized recruitment for the corporates in India. We provide end to end human resource management to various corporate organizations by understanding their needs with our wide range of talent acquisition skill acquired over a decade long service in this industry. We address their critical talent needs by providing comprehensive workforce management from Recruitment Process Outsourcing. In a nutshell we act as a bridge between the campus and corporates creating a win-win situation to all.

Assessment Service

QuanTech Origin provides assessment service in psychometric test, aptitude test and coding test which will save time spent by the recruiters in the initial level evaluation of the candidate. It makes the recruiter job hassle free by taking care of coordination with candidate, follow ups, scheduling, invigilation and other operation stuff. We also provide assessment services to colleges to get their students ready for the placements and other competitive exams by practicing 100+ hours of assessments on all modules.

Campus Recruitment Training

QuanTech Origin has a comprehensive program designed to train students for all the stages of the campus recruitment process done at various engineering and other degree related colleges. The program has different modules for aptitude test, group discussion, and personal interviews. The program is designed to train students in the recruitment process which helps them land a good job in a good company.

Campus Training Process Flow

We believe in a holistic approach of training that involve a plan of action that is executed efficiently by our team of trainers.



The Process Flow includes :-

Pre-Assessment

Pre Assessment process is an unbiased way to gather the standing of students, for further process of imparting a effective and qualitative training. Pre Assessment gives clarity about the knowledge of students and helps us plan agenda accordingly.


The Pre Assessment comprises a set of bridge course questions from: Quantitative Aptitude , Logical Reasoning and Verbal Activity.


A perspective report, based on the performance of a student is generated accordingly and scrutinized.


It is what we know already that often prevents us from learning. - Claude Bernard

Pre Assessment process thus , eliminates the possibility of complacence among students by giving us a report of the level of training the prospective students would require.

Strategy

A vision without a strategy is an illusion.– Lee Bolman

At Quantech , we plan the best strategy based on the Pre Assessment report generated . The strategy involves gauging the potential of students and preparing the training topics accordingly. The trainers are thus, instructed based on whether the students need to be trained at beginner level or at an intermediate level or so.

Training and Transform

Training is a paramount and tremendous process of the working system. We have incorporated the latest of the demand , and check its effectiveness and horizon simultaneously maintaining a student- friendly session and not burden them.


Our training sessions include an intensive training once a week, stretched for 6hrs - for a duration of 10 weeks. Students can garner utmost through his/her complete involvement and deepen their technical proficiency. This methodology adopted also helps us understand the learning curve of the students and adapt accordingly. Our training ensures every student with the absolute sense of his/her learning and self-reliance.

Post Assessment

Post Assessment is an essential step in the entire process , since it unveils the understanding of the students. It gives a crystal clear report of the margin where students can enhance their skill sets.

With the completion of the post assessment process , a closure report is handed over to the college . The report contains the complete information of the training process and an analysis about the Pre and Post Assessment of the student explaining their readiness to placements

Placements

Placement is the significant stage in enhancing an individual’s career. And training paves a very essential part in imposing such an experience. It is very noticeable that placement is the milestone that drives one to the completion of the training program. QuanTech Origin abides by a lot of synergies to spread its network with institutes as well as the corporate industry. We are associated with a lot of competitive firms that are challenging the current market of excellence day by day.
